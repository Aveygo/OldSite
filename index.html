About

My name is Dmitri Shevchenko and I'm a CS undergrad at the University of Technology, Sydney. I spend my days coding and my nights dreaming about algorithms (just kidding... or am I?).

One of my favorite personal projects is a website that I built from scratch. It collects news articles and uses bert encodings and k-means to identify clusters (eg headlines). I'm also currently working on a top-secret project that involves machine learning and lots of coffee.


Aside from coding, I'm into all sorts of geeky stuff. I'm a gamer (shoutout to all my fellow csgo TrashRats!), and love following along r/wallstreetbets and their awful DDs.
